Our Story

Bringing Greenery to Urban Spaces

Welcome to aangan

aangan was founded on a simple principle: everyone deserves a connection to nature. In the hustle and bustle of city life, we saw a need for green sanctuaries, places of calm and growth. We began as a small collective of urban gardening enthusiasts, sharing tips and cuttings from our own balcony gardens.

Today, aangan has blossomed into a community and a resource for urban gardeners across the country. We are dedicated to providing high-quality plants, beautifully designed pots, and all the essentials you need to cultivate your own urban oasis. Our commitment extends to sustainable practices and fostering a community of growers who share our passion.
